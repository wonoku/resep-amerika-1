Healthy Vegan Vegetable Frittata in Microwave instructions

  1. In a non-metallic microwave safe bowl, mix together potatoes, onion, bell peppers, zucchini, garlic, tomatoes and butter..
  2. Cover and microwave on High for 5 minutes, stirring once or twice..
  3. Season with salt and pepper..
  4. Now take a mixing bowl and combine the remaining ingredients listed under batter and whisk well to obtain a smooth, lump free pancake like batter..
  5. Pour this batter/mixture into the prepared vegetable and stir to combine..
  6. Arrange olives on the top surface..
  7. Cover the dish and microwave on high for 3 minutes..
  8. Sprinkle cheese on top, cover and microwave for 1-2 minutes until the cheese melts..
  9. Let it stand for 5 minutes before slicing and serving..
  10. Serve along, with green salad, vegan mayonnaise and with any condiments of choice. Enjoy endlessly..
  11. Note- I have used a 1350 watt microwave.Others may have different wattage. Therefore it might take a little more or less time accordingly..
