Ingredients of Raw jack fruit curry

  12. You need 1 teaspoon of whole jeera.
  13. It's 2-3 of green chillies chopped.
  14. Prepare To taste of salt and sugar.
  15. Prepare As needed of oil.
  16. You need As needed of water.

Raw jack fruit curry step by step

  1. Add oil in a pan fry the Potatoes adding salt and turmeric powder and keep aside..
  2. Now add mote oil in the same pan and temper the dry red chilli whole jeera and bay leaf..
  3. Add the Jack fruit cubes and fry well adding salt and turmeric,keep aside..
  4. Now add all the masalas salt and turmeric powder and fry..
  5. Add tomato puree and saute well.
  6. Now add the fried veggies and saute well adding red chilli Powder.
  7. Add water and cover it for 10 mnts.
  8. Finally add chopped green chillies and sugar and mix well.let it boil.for two three mnts s.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala powder and remove from oven.
  10. Jack fruit curry is ready to serve.
