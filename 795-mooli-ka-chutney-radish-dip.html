Ingredients of Mooli ka chutney (Radish Dip)

  1. Prepare 1 Cup of grated radish.
  2. Prepare 1 Cup of chopped coriander leaves.
  3. Prepare 8 of peeled garlic cloves.
  4. Prepare 4 of medium heat green chilies / to taste.
  5. Prepare 1 of lemon juice.
  6. It's 1 Tbsp of Mustard oil.
  7. It's 3/4 Tsp of salt.
  8. Prepare 1 Tsp of Carom seeds (ajwain).

Mooli ka chutney (Radish Dip) step by step

  1. Take a chutney jar. Put radish, coriander leaves, green chilies, garlic in jar and make fine paste..
  2. Now add salt, lemon juice in chutney and grander for another 1 minute. Take out it in a serving bowl..
  3. Heat oil in a tadka pan or in small kadai. Now add carom seeds and crack for 30 - 40 seconds..
  4. Pour tempared carom seeds along with oil in dip. Mix well..
  5. Spicy and unusal dip is ready to serve..
  6. This dip we can serve with pakodas,and any meal as replacement of pickle..
  7. It could be lasts for longer period if we keep in fridge.
