Jackfruit cutlet instructions

  1. Take a mixing bowl add boiled Jackfruit and boiled potato and mash them properly and mixt them..
  2. Add besan, chopped onion, chopped green chillies, chopped coriander leaves and mix them well..
  3. Add salt, turmeric powder, garam masala powder and mix them well. Shape them according to your wish, I gave circular shape..
  4. Take a pan and heat the oil. Gently keep all the cutlets in to the pan. Fry them from both the sides till they are properly cooked and golden brown..
