Ingredients of Sambhar

  1. It's 1 cup of toor dal.
  2. Prepare 8-10 of cubed pieces Lauki.
  3. It's 3-4 of cubed pieces Brinjal.
  4. You need 1 of medium sized carrot chopped in circular shape.
  5. You need 1 of medium size radish chopped in circular shape.
  6. It's 2 of medium size tomatoes chopped in circular shape.
  7. Prepare 3 cups of water.
  8. It's 1/2 tsp of turmeric powder and salt as per your taste-.
  9. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of tamrind paste (soak tamrind for 20 min and strain).
  10. It's As needed of Curry leaves.
  11. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of sambhar masala.
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp of mustard seeds.
  13. You need 1 of red dry chilli.
  14. You need As needed of ghee.

Sambhar instructions

  1. Boil 1 cup of water in pressure cooker add turmeric powder and salt. Add toor dal, wait for 2-3 whistles on medium flame..
  2. Open the lid when pressure is released and mash properly cooked dal smooth with the help of spoon. Add all chopped vegetables, few curry leaves, 1 cup water,salt and little turmeric powder in the prepared dal. Wait for 1-2 whistles on medium flame. Once pressure is released add sambhar masala, tamrind water and boil for 2-3 minutes..
  3. Heat ghee in pan add mustard seeds, curry leaves and red dry chilli. Give your sambhar a fantastic tadka and serve hot with Idli, Dosa or vadas..
