Ingredients of Grilled Chicken Sandwich

  1. You need of chicken tenders or chicken breast sliced into 1/2 inch strips.
  2. It's of onion sliced into 1/2inch slices. keep the the rings intact for easier grilling.
  3. Prepare of roasted red pepper cut into 1/4 inch strips.
  4. Prepare of garlic cloves minced.
  5. Prepare of extra virgin olive oil.
  6. Prepare of dried oregano.
  7. Prepare of of arugula.
  8. You need of of pepper jack cheese.
  9. It's of ciabatta rolls.
  10. It's of salt & pepper.

Grilled Chicken Sandwich step by step

  1. preheat your grill to medium high for 10 min.
  2. slice ciabatta rolls in half and drizzle with olive oil. season with salt & pepper.
  3. grill rolls until toasted & golden brown.
  4. season both sides of onion slices with salt, pepper & oregano. drizzle with olive oil.
  5. grill onions on both sides just until they have grill marks. about 2 min per side and remove to aluminum foil.
  6. place onions, rossted pepper strips and garlic onto aluminum foil & fold ends together to form a packet.
  7. place packet on the grill while you make the chicken.
  8. season chicken with salt , pepper & oregano. drizzle with olive oil.
  9. grill chicken on each side until an internal temp of 165ยบ and they have nice grill marks.
  10. remove chicken & onion/red pepper packet.
  11. while chicken is resting, roughly chop onions & peppers.
  12. place 2 to 3 chicken strips on the bottom half of each roll & top with a slice of cheese on each.
  13. place on a baking sheet & put them back on the grill until the cheese melts.
  14. remove sandwiches to plates and top with onion/pepper mixture and a handful of arugula.
  15. enjoy!.
