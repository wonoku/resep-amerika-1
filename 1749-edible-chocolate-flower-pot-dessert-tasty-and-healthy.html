Edible chocolate flower pot dessert step by step

  1. Take paper cups and bowls, make a small cut at the top of the cup,so u can easily open the cups after chilling, set aside.
  2. Melt chocolate in a microwave or double boiler,allow to melt completely,stirring occassionaly.
  3. Let it cool for few seconds.
  4. Pour 3-4 tbsp into each cup and bowl and rotate to coat interior, get rid of excess chocolate.
  5. Refrigerate for 15 mins.
  6. Then pour another 2 tbsp and coat the cups get rid of excess chocolate.
  7. Refrigerate while making a filling.
  8. In a medium bowl beat cream cheese, powdered sugar and vanilla essence till smooth. Add heavy cream and whip to medium peaks.
  9. Transfer to a piping bag & chill, u need to fill the cups.
  10. Take out cups frm Refrigerater Open the paper cups.
  11. Place oreo cookies in zip lock bag & crush with a rolling pin.. Keep aside.
  12. To assemble : first add cake pieces at the bottom, next fill the cups with cream cheese mix chopped apple add tutty fruity, chill for atleast one hour.
  13. Then sprinkle with crushed oreos and decorate it with flowers & some mint sprigs.
  14. Note : u can add filling of ur choice.
