Chicken Pan Pie instructions

  1. Boil the chicken with ginger garlic paste salt pepper and green chillies. Keep aside.
  2. Take a kadai add onions and garlic and sautee.Add chopped tomatoes and add all the herbs and spices mentioned in gravy.Let it get thick.Reduce the water.
  3. Shred the chicken and add that.add the stock as well.Reserve some stock to sirtfry the vegges.
  4. STIR FRY VEGGIES: Stir fry the vegetables separately 1 at a time.
  5. Take very little oil add garlic and add the bell peppers add stock and slightly stir fry them with salt and pepper.
  6. Similarly stir fry the Brocolli,Mushrooms and zucchini too.Do not cook the vegetables..
  7. On our gravy spread the sitfried peppers, Mushrooms and broccoli.
  8. Arrange the zucchini on top.
  9. Spread the cheese.
  10. Put the kadai back on stove and cook till the cheese melts.
  11. Serve as it is..
