Whole wheat Apple loaf step by step

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together.
  2. In another bowl, mix the oil, honey and eggs. Then, mix in the apple sauce and milk..
  3. Make a well in the middle of the dry ingredients and add the wet ingredients.
  4. Mix just until well combined. Do not overmix.
  5. Pour into a greased pan and bake on medium heat for about 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center of the loaf comes out clean..
  6. Enjoy your loaf with any beverage of choice or as is..
