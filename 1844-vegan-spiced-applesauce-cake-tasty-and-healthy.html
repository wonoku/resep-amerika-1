Vegan Spiced Applesauce Cake step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350°f.
  2. Grease a 9x5 loaf pan.
  3. Whisk together all dry ingredients.
  4. Whisk together flax eggs, applesauce, both sugar, butter, yogurt and vanilla paste until well combined.
  5. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Do not over mix.
  6. Pour into loaf pan.
  7. Bake for 55-65 mins or until toothpick comes out clean..
  8. Cool for 20 mins and enjoy!.
