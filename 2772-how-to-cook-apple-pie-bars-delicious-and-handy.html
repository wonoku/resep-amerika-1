Ingredients of Apple Pie Bars

  1. Prepare 4 cup of all-purpose flour.
  2. Prepare 1 tsp of salt.
  3. It's 1 tsp of baking powder.
  4. You need 1 cup of shortening.
  5. Prepare 4 of egg yolks.
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of lemon juice.
  7. It's 10 tbsp of cold water.
  8. You need of FILLING:.
  9. You need 7 cup of finely chopped peeled apples.
  10. It's 2 cup of sugar.
  11. You need 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour.
  12. It's 2 tsp of ground cinnamon.
  13. You need 1 dash of ground nutmeg.
  14. Prepare of GLAZE:.
  15. It's 1 cup of powdered sugar.
  16. Prepare 1 tbsp of 2% milk.
  17. You need 1 tbsp of lemon juice.

Apple Pie Bars step by step

  1. Combine flour, salt & baking powder. Cut in shortening until mixture resembles coarse crumbles. In a separate bowl, whisk egg yolks, lemon juice,& water; gradually add to flour mix, tossing with a fork until dough forms a ball. Divide in half & chill for 30 mins..
  2. Roll out one portion of dough & transfer to an ungreased 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan..
  3. In a large bowl, toss apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon & nutmeg; spread over crust. Roll out remaining pastry to fit top of pan; place over filling. Pinch to seal; Cut slits in top..
  4. Bake at 375° for 45-50 minutes or until golden brown. Cool. Combine glaze ingredients until smooth; drizzle over bars before cutting..
