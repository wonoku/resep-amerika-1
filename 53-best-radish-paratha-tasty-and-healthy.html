Radish paratha instructions

  1. First take a bowl add wheat flour, salt mix well then add water make a fine dough....
  2. Then squeeze the grated Radish add salt, red chilli,gram masala, coriander powder, cumin seeds powder, amchur powder, coriander leaves mix well..
  3. Then take dough roll it and cut 2 chef hat shape...then stuffed with stuffing...And lock with the design....
  4. Then Roasted it with oil on tawa..
  5. Serve with sauce...your paratha is ready to serve..
