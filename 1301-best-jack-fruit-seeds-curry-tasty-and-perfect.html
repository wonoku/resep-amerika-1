Jack fruit seeds curry step by step

  1. Cut the vegetables into cubes and the seeds into two halves.
  2. Now heat oil and temper cumin seed bay leaf and green chillies chopped.
  3. As they crackle add the veggies and saute well.
  4. Add salt and turmeric powder and cover till it become soft.
  5. Now add ginger paste and madala powder saute well.
  6. Add water to combine well.
  7. Now add sugar and garam masala powder and mix.
  8. Switch off the flame and serve it with rice.
