Ingredients of Shepherd's pie

  1. It's 1cksp of sunflower/vegetable  oil.
  2. Prepare 1 of large onion.
  3. Prepare 2-3 of carrots.
  4. Prepare 1 of Green/yellow bell peppers.
  5. It's 500 g of pack lamb/beef mince.
  6. You need 1cksp of tomato purée.
  7. You need 100g of potato,cut into chunks.
  8. You need 2tbsp of butter.
  9. It's 1/2cup of milk.
  10. You need 100g of Cheese(Parmesan or mozzarella).

Shepherd's pie instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a pan, then soften the onion, peppers and carrots for a few mins. When soft, turn up the heat, crumble in the lamb/meat and brown, tipping off any excess fat. Add the tomato purée, season to taste then cover and cook..
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oven to 160C/ gas, then make the mash. Boil the potatoes in salted water for 10-15 mins until tender. Drain, then mash with the butter, cheese and milk..
  3. Put the mince sauce into an ovenproof dish, top with the mash and ruffle with a fork. Bake for 20-25 mins until the top is starting to colour and the mince is bubbling through at the edges. Sprinkle cheese on top. Leave to stand for 5 mins before serving..
