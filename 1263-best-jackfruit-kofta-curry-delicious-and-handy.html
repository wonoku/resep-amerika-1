Jackfruit kofta Curry step by step

  1. Take a mixing bowl add mashed jackfruits, potato, green chilli, ginger, garam masala, coriander powder, turmeric powder, salt to taste, red chilli powder, besan & a pinch of baking soda mix well, now make balls of it & deep fry..
  2. Heat a pan add a cup of water, now add all the ingredients for the gravy and bring it to a boil it,cool it & makes a paste. Heat another pan add 2 tbsp oil add 1tsp cumin seeds to crackle for a while, than add chopped onion sauted till it's becomes golden brown..
  3. Put the paste masala sauted to nicely cook gravy. Next add 2cups of water & cover it 5 mintues. Then add 2tbsp cream & jackfruits balls and cook for another 2 mints. Turn off the flame..
  4. Jackfruit Kofta curry is ready to serve. Garnish with cream & coriander leaves, serve it hot..
