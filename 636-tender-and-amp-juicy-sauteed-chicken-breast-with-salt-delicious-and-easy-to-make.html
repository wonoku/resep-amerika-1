Tender & Juicy! Sauteed Chicken Breast with Salt instructions

  1. Slice the chicken breast diagonally into 1-cm thickness. Cut each in half lengthways ( for thin oblongs)..
  2. Add Step 1 into a bowl with sake, sugar, salt, mayonnaise and pepper. Mix well and let it sit for a while..
  3. Add the katakuriko to Step 2 and stir well..
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan and arrange the Step 3 chicken breast. Cover with a lid and cook over medium heat for about 2 minutes..
  5. After the chicken starts to brown, remove the lid. Turn over the chicken pieces and cover with the lid. Continue to cook over a low heat for 1 minute..
  6. Turn the heat off and leave to stand for 1 minute, still covered with the lid..
  7. Remove the lid and add the julienned shiso leaves and transfer to a serving dish..
