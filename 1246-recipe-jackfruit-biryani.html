Jackfruit biryani instructions

  1. Cut jackfruits in cubes and boil for a whistle, deep fry in oil for few minutes..
  2. Heat oil in cooker, add dry masala & saute for few minutes, add onions till turn brown..
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and saute till aroma goes away..
  4. Add rice and saute for few minutes..
  5. Add powder masalas & jackfruit mix well..
  6. Add water to the height of soaking rice..
  7. Cook for one whistle..
  8. Ready to serve..
