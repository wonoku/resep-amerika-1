Ingredients of Ram Laddoo

  1. You need 2 cups of Moong Dal.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of ChanaDal.
  3. You need 2 tbsp of Ginger Paste.
  4. It's 1/4 tsp of Hing(asafoetida).
  5. It's 1 tsp of Red Chilli Powder.
  6. You need 1 pinch of Baking Soda.
  7. It's 2 tsp of Salt.
  8. Prepare as needed of Oil to Fry.
  9. It's of For Garnish.
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp of Chaat Masala.
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp of Mooli / Radish.
  12. You need 1/4 cup of Coriander leaves.
  13. It's as needed of Mint Chutney.
  14. It's as needed of Sweet Tamarind chutney.

Ram Laddoo step by step

  1. Soak both the dals separately at night Grind Soaked moong dal and chanadal into paste using minimal water.
  2. Add ginger paste, cilantro, hing, red chilli powder, baking soda, and salt..
  3. Mix well with hand to make the batter fluffy Heat oil in a wok and put small portions of batter to make ladoos.
  4. Fry until light brown and crispy..
  5. For Garnishing Top it with some chaat masala, grated mooli, Coriander leaves, green chutney and sweet tamarind chutney and serve!.
