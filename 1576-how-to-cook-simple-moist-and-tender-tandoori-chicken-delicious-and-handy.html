Ingredients of Simple, Moist, and Tender Tandoori Chicken

  1. Prepare of Chicken breast.

Simple, Moist, and Tender Tandoori Chicken step by step

  1. Poke the chicken with a fork so that the flavor can seep in easily..
  2. Add all of the ingredients into a plastic bag and rub it all together from the outside of the bag..
  3. Add the chicken and massage the flavors into the meat..
  4. Squeeze out the air from the bag and close it. Place in the refrigerator overnight..
  5. Dump the chicken and the sauce onto a heat resistant dish. (Caution: Since the chicken is cold, there's a chance that they won't cook all the way through, so please be sure to heat them up in the microwave first).
  6. Bake in the toaster oven for 15-20 minutes until golden brown. (Adjust the time based on the color).
  7. (Instead of a toaster oven, you can use a frying pan or the fish grill. You could also use the oven.).
  8. Cut into easy to eat slices and arrange on a plate. It's done!.
