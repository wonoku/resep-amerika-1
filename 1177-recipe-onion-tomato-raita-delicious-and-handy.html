Onion tomato raita instructions

  1. Rinse the onion, tomato, mint leaves and green chillI..
  2. Finely chop them..
  3. In a bowl whisk 1 cup curd /dahi /yogurt till smooth..
  4. Add finely chopped onion to the whisked curd..
  5. Add finely chopped tomato..
  6. Add 2-3 tsp chopped mint/pudina & 1 finely chopped green chilli, instead of mint leaves you can also add chopped coriander leaves/dhania patta or a mix of both..
  7. Add 1 tsp roasted cumin powder,1/8 tsp red chilli powder and salt as required..
  8. Mix the raita well..
  9. Serve onion tomato raita with PULAO, BIRYANI or with other rice dishes.if you want you can even garnish this raita with a few mint or coriander leaves.I have garnish with some mint powder also for a tasty and yummy flavour..
