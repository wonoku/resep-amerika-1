Ingredients of Loaded Green Tomatoes

  1. It's of green tomatoes.
  2. You need of flour.
  3. You need of eggs.
  4. Prepare of hidden valley ranch.
  5. Prepare of Sunflower/Avocado oil.
  6. Prepare of lemon juice.
  7. You need of boisin sauce.
  8. You need of Avocado.
  9. You need of crushed pepper flakes.
  10. Prepare of adobe seasoning.

Loaded Green Tomatoes instructions

  1. Slice tomatoes.
  2. Mix flour and ranch together.
  3. Beat eggs in seperate bowl.
  4. Pour oil in frying pan..heat on high for about a minute then turn down to medium heat.
  5. Take tomatoe slices and dip in flour/ranch mix..then dip in egg and once again in flour/ranch mix.
  6. Place slices in pan and fry on both sides til golden brown.
  7. When done take the sliced green tomates and place on a paper towel.
  8. Take a avocado in a bowl,add a splash of lemon juice,season with adobe seasoning...smash it all together and add some crushed pepper flakes.Place on top of green tomatoes.
  9. Garnish with cherry tomatoes if you like.
  10. Lightly drizzle with boisin sauce.
