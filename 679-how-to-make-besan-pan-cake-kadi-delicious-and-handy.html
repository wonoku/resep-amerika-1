Besan pan cake kadi step by step

  1. Let's prepare pancakes..take a bowl put besin,salt,turmeruc,curd,chilli paste,coriander.
  2. Mix well pour water to make pan cake batter keep mix as lumpfree batter kind.
  3. Heat a tava apply oil pour a batter like but little thickness add oil fry both sides once it's roasted turn another side fry well.
  4. Take out pan cake cut square pieces keep a side.
  5. Heat a kadahi pour oil add mustard,cumin seeds when it's splits add Onion,chilli,add tomato pieces.
  6. Pour half cup water keep boil add coriander,garam masala,dhaniya powder,sugar,salt,chilli powder all the flavours should boiled and forms gravy.
  7. Then add pan cake pieces mix up gently close lid 2 mins once cake pieces binds flavour take out the kadi garnish with coriander ready to serve.
