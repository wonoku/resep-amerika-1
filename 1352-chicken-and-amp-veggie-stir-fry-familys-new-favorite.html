Chicken & veggie stir-fry instructions

  1. Heat oil add chicken cook about 5 to 8 minutes add seasoning to taste.
  2. Now add veggies and mix into chicken, add your stir-fry sauce cover and simmer for 5 minutes. Veggies should be tender not soft..
  3. Cook chow mein noodles according to package directions..
  4. Plate noodles and top with chicken and vegetables enjoy. ..
