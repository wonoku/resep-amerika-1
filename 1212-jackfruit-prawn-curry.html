Jackfruit prawn curry instructions

  1. At first cut the jackfruit and Potatoes into cubed.
  2. Now marinate the prawns salt and turmeric powder.
  3. Add oil in a pan and shallow fry the prawns,keep aside.
  4. Now fry the Potatoes and jackfruit,keep aside.
  5. Now add oil and ghee in the pan and temper the dry red chilli whole jeera and bay leaf.
  6. Saute the Onions with salt and turmeric powder.
  7. Add ginger garlic paste saute well till the raw smell disappears.
  8. Add jeera,dhaniya and red chilli Powder mix well.
  9. Add tomato pureed mix well.
  10. Now add the fried veggies and prawns combine well.
  11. Add water and cover it for 10 mimutes.
  12. Finally add garam masala and switch off the flame.
  13. Jackfruit prawn curry is ready to be served.
