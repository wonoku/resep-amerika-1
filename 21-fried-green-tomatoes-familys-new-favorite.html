Ingredients of Fried green tomatoes

  1. You need of tomatoes.
  2. You need 3 medium of green tomatoes.
  3. You need 1/3 cup of milk.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of all purpose flour.
  5. Prepare 2 of beaten eggs.
  6. Prepare 3/4 of fine bread crumbs.
  7. You need 1/4 cup of cooking oil.

Fried green tomatoes step by step

  1. Cut tomatoes into 1" slices. Sprinkle with salt and pepper..
  2. Dip slices in milk, then flour, then eggs, then bread crumbs..
  3. Before frying, its best to put the coated tomato slices in the freezer for 2 hours. The coating will stay on better. Fry the slices in hot oil over med heat 8-10 mins each side or until brown..
  4. Season with salt and pepper and serve..
