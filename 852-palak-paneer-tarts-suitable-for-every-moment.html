Palak Paneer Tarts instructions

  1. Heat oil and add the cumin seeds.when the seeds crackle add the onions and fry till translucent.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste and green chillies and all the dry spices mentioned.
  3. Saute and add the tomatoes and let it cook till mushy.
  4. Add the spinach leaves and cook till the leaves change their colour.
  5. Let it cool and then grind the mixture into a smooth paste.Do not add water.
  6. In the same pan add the puree and add cubed Paneer.Cook for 5 to 7 mins.
  7. Take the flour add the sugar and the salt and add the cold butter.
  8. Rub the butter into the flour and now add cold water and make a very stiff dough..
  9. Refrigerate the dough for atleast 30 mins.
  10. Take the dough out and spread it.Make small roundels and place each of it in a mini tart pan.make a thin layer. Pierce holes in the tart and bake the tarts @ 200 The Celsius for 20 mins in a preheated oven.
  11. Fill the tarts with our palak paneer gravy.Add some cheese on top and bake again for 5 mins.
  12. Enjoy the mini tarts guilt free..
