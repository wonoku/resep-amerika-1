Bengali mix vegetables step by step

  1. Heat oil and fry lentil dumplings.
  2. Remove and add oil to temper five spices,bay leaf and green chillies paste.
  3. Now add the vegetables and saute for a while.
  4. Add salt and turmeric powder and cover.
  5. Add ginger paste and saute until the veggies are soft.
  6. Add roasted masala and coconut mix.
  7. Add lentil dumplings and sugar cover it till every get combined.
  8. Serve it hot and enjoy.
