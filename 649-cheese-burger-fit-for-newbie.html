Cheese Burgerüçî instructions

  1. For tomato jam - this is to replace ketchup Put the veggies on a hot pan Add spices and let it cook for 5 mins Add the water and let it simmer until all the water has evaporated At the end it should resemble a fruit jam Set aside to cool.
  2. Patty Add all the spices except for black pepper and salt mix until all blended well Scoop a handful of the minced meat make a round ball and make sure the meat is 1 inch bigger than the bun then press it down to form a patty shape At the centre press the meat down to make a well so as to ensure the roundness is maintained during cooking Season with black pepper and salt Place on very hot oiled pan at medium high heat preferably a iron skillet and cook meat for 4 minutes on each side..
  3. Continuation On one side add 2 slices of cheddar cheese cover with a lid for the top part to melt really well for 1 minute Set aside.
  4. Toast bun Spread butter on each bun and toast on same skillet for 1-2 minutes each on low medium heat to get a golden colour.
