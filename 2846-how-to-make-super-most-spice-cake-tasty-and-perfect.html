Super most spice cake step by step

  1. Pre heat the oven to 350°f and 177°c.
  2. Spread butter in a Baking tin.
  3. Prepare all ingredients.
  4. Whisk the flour,baking powder,baking soda,salt and other spices together in a large bowl set a side..
  5. Whisk oil,brown sugar,apple sauce,eggs,vanilla essence together in a bowl..
  6. Pour the wet ingredients and whisk until combined.
  7. Spread batter in to the prepared tin.
  8. Baked for 45 minutes.
  9. Cake is done when a toothpick inserted in the centre comes out clean.
  10. Remove cake from oven and set on a wire track.
  11. Allow to cool.
