Ingredients of Es Teler (Indonesian Fruit Drink)

  1. You need 1 of young coconut, rip out the meat with a spoon.
  2. Prepare 2 of ripe avocados, cut into dice.
  3. Prepare 100 gr of jackfruit, cut into diced.
  4. It's 6 tablespoons of sweetened condensed milk.
  5. You need 6 tablespoons of cocopandan syrup.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of coconut cream.
  7. It's of shaved ice.

Es Teler (Indonesian Fruit Drink) instructions

  1. In a small pot, stir the coconut cream with a pinch of salt constantly until boiling. Set aside. Let it cool..
  2. Put the young coconut meat, avocado, and jackfruit in a glass or a bowl..
  3. Add the coconut milk, cocopandan syrup, and sweetened condensed milk. Put the shaved ice on top. Ready to be served! ðŸ˜‹.
