Skye's applesauce oatmeal cookies instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. cream together first 5 ingredients.
  3. to that mixture add the last 3 ingredients and blend well.
  4. at this point you can add optional ingredients such as... 1/2 cup raisins, or 1/2 cup walnuts, or 1/2 cup chocolate chips , etc..
  5. drop cookies by teaspoonfuls onto an ungreased cookie sheet and bake 10 - 12 minutes.
  6. transfer baked cookies to cooling rack and allow to cool completely.
  7. makes approximately 4 dozen cookies.
