Jaickfruit sabzi instructions

  1. Take Pressure cooker,putmustard oil, keep warm for frying jackfruit..
  2. Fry jackfruit prices and keep aside..
  3. Put tadka ingredients in rest of the oil after crackle put desi ghee- in it.
  4. After cracking tadka add chopped onion cook till golden brown.
  5. Add gingergarlic paste &cook for 2 min.
  6. Add all dry spices and little beat water...Cook til when spicesleave the oil..
  7. Add jackfruit mix well for 2 min and add 1cup of water.
  8. Pressure cook for 2 whistle..
  9. Once it cool garnish with green corriender and serve with chapati or rice..
