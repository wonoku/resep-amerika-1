Apple caramel cookies instructions

  1. For the caramel, melt and combine all ingredients in a saucepan over medium heat stirring occasionally until it comes to a rolling boil..
  2. For the cookie dough, cream butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar. Mix in the vanilla extract and egg. In a separate bowl, combine flour, cinnamon, and baking soda. Gradually add to flour mixture. Stir in apples..
  3. Drop cookie dough by rounded teaspoons onto a greased cookie sheet. Drizzle carmel sauce over the cookies. Bake 8 to 10 minutes at 350°F or until slightly brown..
  4. They will stay soft for weeks! Enjoy!.
