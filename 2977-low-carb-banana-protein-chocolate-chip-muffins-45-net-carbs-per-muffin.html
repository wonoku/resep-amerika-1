Ingredients of Low Carb Banana Protein Chocolate Chip Muffins - ( 4.5 net carbs per muffin)

  1. Prepare of Muffins.
  2. You need 3 tbsp of Bob's Red Mill Low Carb Baking Mix (6 net carbs).
  3. You need 1/4 cup of organic steel cut oats (23 net carbs).
  4. It's 2 of scoop of Isopure banana or chocolate protein powder (I use this brand because it is so low carb, but there are others out there.) (1 net carbs).
  5. It's 1 tbsp of baking powder.
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp of baking soda.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of ground cinnamon.
  8. It's 1/4 cup of unsweetened apple sauce (5.5 net carbs).
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup of non-fat plain, banana or vanilla flavored yogurt (I use 1 tube of Chobani snack size tubes - banana flavor, 7 net carbs).
  10. Prepare 2 of large eggs (2 net carbs).
  11. Prepare 6 tbsp of melted cooled coconut oil or butter (add 4 TBSP to batter and drizzle remaining 2 TBSP evenly into the 12 cups after you have sprayed the pan with non-stick spray) (0 net carbs).
  12. It's 1 tbsp of banana flavored emulsion or extract (0 net carbs).
  13. It's 6 tbsp of ***Hershey sugar-free dark chocolate chips (add to the top of each muffin just before cooking)*** (6 net carbs).
