Taco Pie step by step

  1. Heat oven to 375°.
  2. unroll dough; separate into 8 triangles. place in 10 inch ungreased pie plate.
  3. press on bottom and up sides to form a crust..
  4. cook beef throughly, drain.
  5. stir in taco seasoning and salsa; simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. spoon cooked beef into pie pan and sprinkle with cheese.
  7. Bake 14-17 minutes or until crust is a deep golden brown and cheese is melted.
  8. take out of oven and top with shredded lettuce and tomatoes.
  9. add sour cream, crushed up doritos or taco sauce and enjoy!.
