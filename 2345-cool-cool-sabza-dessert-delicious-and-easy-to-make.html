Cool cool sabza dessert instructions

  1. First for reduced milk boil 500 ml of full fat milk along with few strands of saffron.
  2. Cook it in a heavy bottomed pan.
  3. Let it cook on low flame, stirring occasionally.
  4. When the quantity of milk becomes 1/4 then add 2 tbsp of sugar.
  5. Let it cook further for 5 - 7 minutes.
  6. When done take it out in a bowl and let it cool down.
  7. When completely cools down then keep it in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
  8. Soak sabza seeds in a cup of water for 3 - 4 hours, it will swell up to four times.
  9. For assembling take two tall glasses.
  10. In one glass first put 1 tbsp of rose syrup.
  11. Then put 1 tbsp of sabza seeds.
  12. Then put 3 tbsp of reduced milk.
  13. Then on top place some mixed chopped fruits.
  14. Now pour khus syrup on top of the fruits.
  15. In the same way assemble the other glass also.
  16. But in the second glass, put the khus syrup in the base and rose syrup on top.
  17. The cool cool dessert is ready to serve.
