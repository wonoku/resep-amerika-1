Ingredients of Yellowtail with Shio-Koji Steamed in a Microwave

  1. You need 2 large of Yellow tail (buri).
  2. You need 1 tbsp of ★Sake.
  3. You need 2 tsp of ★Shio-koji (salt-fermented rice malt).
  4. It's 5 of cm Daikon radish.
  5. You need 1/3 of Carrot.
  6. Prepare 1 stalk of Green onion.
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp of ☆Sake.
  8. You need 2 tsp of ☆Garlic soy sauce.
  9. Prepare 1 tsp of ☆Sesame oil.

Yellowtail with Shio-Koji Steamed in a Microwave step by step

  1. Chop each yellow tail into 3 pieces, and marinate in the ★ ingredients for 15 minutes. Julienne the daikon radish and carrot, then cut the green onion into thin round slices..
  2. Arrange the daikon radish and carrot in a heatproof dish, and swirl in the ☆ ingredients. Place the yellow tail on top, loosely cover with plastic wrap, and microwave for 4-5 minutes..
  3. When finished cooking, sprinkle with the green onion..
