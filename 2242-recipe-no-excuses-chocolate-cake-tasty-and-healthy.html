No excuses chocolate cake step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Spray bundt pan and dust with chocolate PDM.
  3. Stir dry ingredients together in large bowl.
  4. Add wet ingredients, except boiling water (remember with flax seed, prepare it in water before adding to the mixture.
  5. Beat for 2 minutes.
  6. Stir in boiling water. Batter will be thin.
  7. Pour batter into pan and bake for 30 minutes.
  8. Cool completely.
  9. Serve with whip cream out of a can since it has no High Fructose Corn Syrup. Add sliced strawberries or other fruit and enjoy!.
