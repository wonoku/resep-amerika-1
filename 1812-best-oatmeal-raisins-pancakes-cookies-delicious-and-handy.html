Ingredients of Oatmeal raisins Pancakes Cookies

  1. Prepare of Banana.
  2. Prepare of Eggs.
  3. Prepare of Baking powder.
  4. You need of Vanilla extract.
  5. Prepare of Crunchy peanut butter.
  6. Prepare of Applesauce.
  7. You need of Almond milk.
  8. You need of Rolled oats.
  9. It's of Whole wheat flour.
  10. It's of Raisins.

Oatmeal raisins Pancakes Cookies instructions

  1. In a medium bowl mash the banana and then mix in the baking powder.
  2. Add in egg, vainilla, peanut butter, applesauce, and almond milk whisking to combine..
  3. Next goes the oats and flour completely. Then toss in the raisins and give another few mixes..
  4. Heat a skillet and pour 1/4 cup of pancake mix to make a pancake. Let cake cook for 2-3 minutes or until bubbles start to form and pop on top of batter. Flip cake and let cook for another 1-2 minutes..
  5. Stack cookies pancakes and top with whipped cream and syrup!!!! Enjoy.
