Spice Cake step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees centigrade and grease baking tin.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg and cloves.
  3. Whisk oil, brown sugar, applesauce, eggs and vanilla.
  4. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and whisk until well combined.
  5. Fold in grated apple.
  6. Spread batter in prepared baking tin and bake 40-45 min..
  7. NB: To make apple sauce.
  8. Peel and cut 4 or 5 apples into pieces and place into a pan. Sprinkle with 1/2 tsp of cinnamon. Add a cup of water and bring to the boil for about 15 mins.
  9. When soft mash into a pulp with a potato masher or put into a blender to get the apple puree..
