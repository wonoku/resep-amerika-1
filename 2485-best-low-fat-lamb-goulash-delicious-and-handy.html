low fat lamb goulash instructions

  1. sautee the onion and garlic with a small amount of oil in a non stick pan until browned a bit..
  2. add the lamb and season with salt and pepper. cook until meat sealed..
  3. sprinkle with the flour and paprika and let it cook out for 1 minute so that it releases the flavour.
  4. Add the lamb stock.
  5. add the rest of the ingredients and cover..
  6. you can either simmer for 1 hour until potatoes and meat are tender or transfer to slow cooker-crock pot..
  7. If on the slow cooker cook on high for 1 hour and reduce to low and caary on cooking for 3-4 hours..
  8. To make it low fat you can skim the floating fat at the end. It will depend on how lean your lamb was..
