Ingredients of Crispy soya

  1. It's 1 cup of Dried soya (soaked in the hot water for 20 mins).
  2. Prepare 4 of Sun dried tomatoes.
  3. It's 2 tsp of Chilli powder.
  4. It's 1 tsp of Turmeric powder.
  5. You need 2 tsp of Garam masala powder.
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp of Corn flour.
  7. You need 2 tbsp of Ginger garlic paste.
  8. It's 1 tbsp of Sweet soya sauce.
  9. It's 1 tbsp of Apple cider vinegar.
  10. Prepare 4 tbsp of Olive oil.
  11. Prepare 1 cup of Water.
  12. You need to taste of Salt.

Crispy soya instructions

  1. In a pan pour oil and add soya, chilli powder, turmeric powder and garam masala powder..
  2. Stir often and fry for about 10min. Now add ginger garlic paste and cook for another 5mins..
  3. Now add water, (if you need to add another 1/2 cup more water) Cover and cook in low flame until soya becomes soft..
  4. Now add tomato, soya sauce, vinegar, salt and cook until the water absorbed completely. Now add cornflour and keep stirring it for 5 min and remove it..
  5. Your crispy soya is now ready to serve with fried rice and chappati..
