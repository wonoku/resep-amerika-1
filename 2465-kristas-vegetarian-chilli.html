Kristas vegetarian chilli step by step

  2. throw garlic in with onions diced up and peppers.
  3. add some garlic salt to veggies.
  4. add corn.
  5. mix good and add beans in.
  6. heat for 5 minuets.
  7. throw in diced tomato.
  8. put salsa in.
  9. add 2 cups of,water or your preferred thickness.
  10. add diced fresh tomato.
  11. add packet of McCormick chilli seasoning.
  12. stir and turn down to low.
  13. let simmer 2 to 4 hours.
  14. put in bowl and throw some shredded cheese on top and dip with tortilla chips.
