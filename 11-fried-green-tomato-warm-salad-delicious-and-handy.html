Fried green tomato warm salad step by step

  1. In a pan heat the margarine. When melted add zucchini sliced in medallions..
  2. Chop the radicchio and fennel bulb. Add to zucchini. Add the spices..
  3. Chop the green tomato stir fry. After 2 minutes add 1 tablespoons of red wine vinegar. When the liquid dissappears, its done.
  4. Dress with olive oil and red wine vinegar.
