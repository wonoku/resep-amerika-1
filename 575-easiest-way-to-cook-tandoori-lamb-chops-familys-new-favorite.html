Ingredients of Tandoori Lamb Chops

  1. Prepare 6 of Lamb Chops.
  2. You need 1/2 cup of yogurt.
  3. It's 2 tbsps of ginger paste.
  4. You need 2 tbsps of garlic paste.
  5. It's 1 tbsp of garam masala.
  6. It's 1 tsp of coriander powder.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of chilli powder.
  8. You need to taste of salt.
  9. Prepare a pinch of of red food coloring (optional).

Tandoori Lamb Chops step by step

  1. Mix all the ingredients excluding the lamb chops together in a bowl. Add the lamb chops and mix together well so that the chops are fully coated with the marinde.
  2. Place in the fridge for a few hours or over night.
  3. When ready to cook, place straight on the bbq or if you are cooking in an oven, cook in a preheated oven at 180C.
  4. Serve it with a mint sauce. Absolutely yum!.
