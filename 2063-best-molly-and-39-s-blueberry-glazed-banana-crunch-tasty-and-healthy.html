Molly's blueberry glazed banana crunch step by step

  1. Crush 1 cup fruity puffs in 8" baking pan.
  2. melt 2 tbsp butter an 2 tbsp sugar together in bowl.
  3. Slice 2 bananas thinly over a crumbled layer of puffs while sprinkling your butter through the mixture (take a spoon an mix up the mix to make sure bananas are coated).
  4. Crumble the other cup of puffs over the mix an pour the rest of the butter evenly over the mix..
  5. Sprinkle marshmallow minis over the mix and thinly slice your last banana.
  6. Preheat oven to 350 and pop your pan in for about 5 minutes..
  7. In a sauce pan mix all blueberry glaze ingredients together an cook on medium heat until its all melted down.
  8. Apply to half of your banana mix (if you like) an pop back in the oven for 5 more minutes.
  9. Let it cool for 5 minutes and enjoy!.
