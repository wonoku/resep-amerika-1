Delhi Style Ram Ladoo instructions

  1. Firstly rinse all Dal then soak for 4-5 hours. Now drain the water add soak dal in blender.Add ginger, Green chilli, pinch of Hing and coriander leaves, make smooth batter and keep aside..
  2. For the Green, Chutney take coriander leaves, Green chilli, ginger, dry Mango, Hing, Lemon juice, Radish leaves, Salt and water in a grinder and make smooth chutney..
  3. For methi chutney, soak methi 5-6 hours. Take a pan turn on the gas on medium flame with water and boil it. Now add methi, Hing Mango powder, coriander seeds, turmeric, Red chilli powder and Fennel seeds,Salt covered it for 20-25 minutes. After 25 minutes turn off the flame and keep aside. After 20 minutes make a smooth chutney..
  4. Now transfer the batter to the large bowl add salt, beat and mix well with hand until the batter change the colour,now start dropping batter as a vada to hot oil. Fry vada till they turn drown colour..
  5. Now vada is ready, place the vada into serving plate, on the top add both chutney, chat masala, coriander leaves, grated radish salt and lemon juice and Enjoy..
