Ingredients of chicken and broccoli

  1. It's 1 packages of chicken tenderloin.
  2. Prepare 1/2 of onion.
  3. It's 1 packages of frozen broccoli.
  4. It's 1 can of carrots.
  5. Prepare 2 cup of Minute Rice.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of soy sauce.
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp of cornstarch.

chicken and broccoli instructions

  1. mix you're soy sauce and corn starch together and set aside.
  2. put two cups of water in a pot. let come to a boil. add 2 cups of Minute Rice and take off the burner and put a lid over the pot.
  3. Saute the chicken tender loins.
  4. add water when needed.
  5. when chicken is almost done take out and cut up into small pieces then put back into the pan to finish cooking.
  6. in a separate pan saute half of an onion.
  7. add frozen broccoli and one can of carrots to the chicken.
  8. add the saute onion to the chicken.
  9. add your soy sauce and cornstarch mixture to the chicken.
