Vegan chipotle chilli step by step

  1. Add olive oil, onions, carrots and garlic to a large pot (at least 5 quart or larger). Cook on medium heat until onions are slightly translucent.
  2. Add the chopped green and red pepper. Cook until vegetables are slightly tender.
  3. Add sliced mushrooms and cook for another 2 to 3 mins.
  4. Rinse the canned beans and corn under cool running water. Add to mixture.
  5. Stir in all remaining ingredients. Blend the chipotle peppers with adobo sauce before adding to the chilli..
  6. Bring the chilli to a boil then reduce heat and simmer. Cook for 45 mins to an hour until chilli has thickened and the beans/vegetables are tender. Add more vegetable stock of the chilli becomes too dry.
