Ingredients of Crispy fried green tomatoes

  1. It's 2 cups of flour.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of cornstarch.
  3. It's 2 tablespoons of pepper.
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon of salt.
  5. It's Dash of hot pepper flakes.
  6. You need 3 of large green tomatoes.
  7. You need of Vegetable oil for frying.
  8. It's 1/2 of milk.
  9. You need 1 of large egg.
  10. Prepare Dash of garlic salt.
  11. It's 1/3 cup of Italian bread crumbs.

Crispy fried green tomatoes instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, pepper, hot flakes,cornstarch, and bread crumbs in large bowl.
  2. Slice tomatoes.
  3. In another bowl mix egg and milk.
  4. I use a cast iron pan but deep fryer or frying pan will work , heat oil on med heat you want it hot before you put your tomatoes in.
  5. When oil is ready dip tomato slices into mixture then into four mixture cover completely in flour mixture press flour mix into the slices then lightly shake off and put into hot oil.
  6. Fry until deep golden brown flip and repeat.
  7. I add a dash of garlic salt on the top when all finished !.
  8. Eat while hot and enjoy!!!!!.
