Palak paneer step by step

  1. Wash palak and cut in 1 inches.
  2. Cook the palak, radish for 2 whistles in pressure cooker.
  3. Blend the palak in smooth paste.
  4. Take a kadai heat ghee add jeera, adrak, chillies when it crackled add onion and saute till brown. add tomatoes and cook till soft.
  5. Add rest of ingredients and cook 5 minutes more. Serve hot.
