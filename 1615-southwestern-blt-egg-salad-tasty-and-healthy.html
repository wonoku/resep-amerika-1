Ingredients of Southwestern BLT Egg Salad

  1. It's of For Salad.
  2. Prepare 4 of large Boston lettuce leaves.
  3. You need 4 cups of chopped blend of iceburg and romaine lettece, purple cabbage and carrots.
  4. Prepare 1 of cucumber, sliced.
  5. You need 1 of radish, sliced.
  6. It's 1 of red onion, sliced.
  7. It's 1-2 of tomatoes, cut in wedges.
  8. You need 6 slices of crispy cooked bacon.
  9. It's 4 of hard boiled eggs, cut in halve, sprinkled with southwestern seasoning.
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup of finely shredded Monterey jack cheese.
  11. Prepare 1/4 cup of crushed blue and white tortilla chips.
  12. It's of For Dressing.
  13. It's 1 of recipe my spicy Ranch Dip/z Dressing with 1 tablespoon southwestern seasoning blended in. My dressing can be found in my profile and in search.
  14. Prepare 1 of lime, wedged for adding as desired.

Southwestern BLT Egg Salad step by step

  1. Lay Boston lettece on serving plate. Put chopped salad in the center.
  2. Add cucumbers and radishes around edges.
  3. Add tomatos and red onion.
  4. Add eggs.
  5. Add cheese and tortillas.
  6. Finally add bacon and lime wedges.
  7. Drizzle a small amount of dressing over and pass more to add to each serving as needed, adding a bit of fresh lime.
