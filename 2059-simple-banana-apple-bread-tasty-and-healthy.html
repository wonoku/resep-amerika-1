Simple Banana Apple Bread step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease 3x 8x4 tins..
  2. Mash bananas..
  3. Mix together wet ingredients with the mashed bananas..
  4. Mix dry ingredients together in a separate bowl..
  5. Stir in wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Do not over blend..
  6. Fill tins evenly and bake for 1 hour and 10 minutes. Check with a tooth pick starting around the 50 minute mark. When the toothpick comes out clean, they're done. Let cool..
  7. (Freeze) Wrap in Cling wrap then a layer of tinfoil to freeze. Lasts up to 6 months in a deep freeze. Thaw out overnight in the fridge..
  8. ~Homemade Upgrade~ Use my recipe to make the Applesauce yourself..
  9. +Taste Replace+ Use leftover fruit baby puree or older, soft fruit cooked and mashed instead of applesauce..
  10. *Health Hint* Substitute white sugar with more applesauce and a bit more flour..
