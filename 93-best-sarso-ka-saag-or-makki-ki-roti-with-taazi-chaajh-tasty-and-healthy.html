Sarso ka saag or makki ki roti with taazi chaajh instructions

  1. Add the three saags, salt and water into pressure cooker and cook over low heat for 1 hour..
  2. Squeeze out saag and keep saag water aside. Mash saag in the cooker until coarsely ground..and add Makki atta and stir...
  3. Put back saag water and a little fresh water and boil over slow heat.
  4. For tadka take pan and heat the oil and add zeera,chopped onion, tomatoes,ginger,red chilli powder saute until masala come are light brown colour. Add the garam masala on it..
  5. Mix into saag and saag is ready..
  6. Take grated radish and makki atta in a big bowl..
  7. Knead the makki atta until it becomes a ball,add atta to dry it and knead..
  8. Heat the tava and add a little ghee so that it does not stick..
  9. Make round shaped of makki roti on chakla and carefully transfer to the tava.
  10. Cook with ghee till golden brown..
  11. Serve with hot sarso da saag, fresh chaajh and salad..
