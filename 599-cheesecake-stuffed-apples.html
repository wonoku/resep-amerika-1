Ingredients of Cheesecake stuffed apples

  1. You need 4 tbsp of melted butter.
  2. You need 2 tbsp of brown sugar.
  3. You need 1 tsp of cinnamon.
  4. Prepare 4 of baking apples, tops sliced, insides scooped.
  5. You need 2 blocks of cream cheese, softened.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of powdered sugar.
  7. It's 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract.
  8. Prepare of crushed graham for dusting.

Cheesecake stuffed apples step by step

  1. Heat oven to 350°.
  2. In small bowl mix together butter, brown sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Placed apples in baking dish and brush on apples. Bake for 20 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile in large bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until creamy. Spoon mixture into stuffed apples and bake for 10 mins..
  5. Garnish with cinnamon and crushed graham crackers.
