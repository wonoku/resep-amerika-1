Ingredients of Crockpot pork green chile stew

  1. You need 2 lb of pork roast,fat trimmed.
  2. Prepare 1 medium of chopped yellow onion.
  3. It's 2 clove of garlic,minced.
  4. You need 1 clove of garlic,thin sliced.
  5. It's 4 oz of can diced green chiles.
  6. It's 2 slice of jalepeno,slice and deseed.
  7. You need 2 cup of chicken broth.
  8. You need 8 oz of can diced tomato do not drain.
  9. It's 8 oz of can tomato with chiles.
  10. You need 1 tsp of oregano.
  11. You need 1/2 tsp of cumin.
  12. It's 1/2 tsp of sage.
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp of paprika.
  14. You need 1/2 tsp of cayenne pepper.

Crockpot pork green chile stew instructions

  1. Place pork in crockpot and add onion and garlic and jalepno all around pork..
  2. Pour on broth,chiles and tomatoes.
  3. Toss spices on top and cook on low 6-8 hrs.
  4. Shred pork when done cooking and serve..
