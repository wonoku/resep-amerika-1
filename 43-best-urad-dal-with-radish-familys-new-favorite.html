Ingredients of Urad dal with radish

  1. It's 1 of large radish cut into rings.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of boiled urad dal.
  3. You need 1 tsp of nigella seeds.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of turmeric powder.
  5. You need 1 of red chilli whole.
  6. Prepare To taste of salt and sugar.
  7. You need As needed of oil.

Urad dal with radish instructions

  1. Heat oil and temper nigella seeds and red chilli.
  2. Add radish sliced and saute well adding salt and turmeric powder.
  3. Add boiled dal and simmer till the radish is soft.
  4. Add sugar and boil well,serve with rice..
