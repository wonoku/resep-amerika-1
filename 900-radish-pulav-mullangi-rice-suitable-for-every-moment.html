Ingredients of Radish Pulav/Mullangi Rice

  1. It's of Radish.
  2. Prepare of Big onion-1no.
  3. It's of Tomato-2nos.
  4. It's of Greenchillies.
  5. Prepare of Gingergarlic paste-1tbsp.
  6. Prepare of Shahijeera or jeera-1teaspoon.
  7. Prepare of Cinnamon stick-1inch.
  8. You need of Cardamom-2nos.
  9. It's of Cloves-5nos.
  10. It's of Mint leaves or coriander leaves-1sprig.
  11. Prepare of Bread slices-3nos.
  12. It's of Greenpeas-1/2cup.
  13. Prepare of Salt.
  14. It's of Oil.
  15. You need of Cooked rice.

Radish Pulav/Mullangi Rice instructions

  1. Cook rice with salt and keep aside.
  2. In a pan add 5tbsp cooking oil.
  3. Add shahijeera.
  4. Once it splutters add cinnamon stick.
  5. Add cardamom and cloves.
  6. Add sliced onion and saute.
  7. Add turmeric powder.
  8. Squeeze and add grated radish mix well and cover and cook in a medium flame.
  9. Now add fresh green peas.
  10. Grind tomato and green chilli into a fine paste.
  11. Now add the paste into a pan and saute well.
  12. Now add required salt.
  13. Add fresh mint leaves or coriander.
  14. Add cooked rice and mix well..
  15. You can add fried bread pieces for extra taste but its optional.
