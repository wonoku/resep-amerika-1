Ingredients of Steamed Chicken Tenders and Cabbage with Yuzu Pepper Paste

Steamed Chicken Tenders and Cabbage with Yuzu Pepper Paste instructions

  1. Prep: Tear the cabbage up into bite-sized pieces. Cut the ginger into thin strips. Chop the scallions as well..
  2. Slice the chicken tender diagonally and spread the yuzu pepper paste..
  3. In a microwave safe plate, spread the cabbage and arrange the chicken and ginger. Sprinkle with sake..
  4. Cover with plastic wrap and microwave for 2-3 minutes. Just enough to cook through the chicken..
  5. Garnish with the scallions. Pour over the ponzu sauce..
