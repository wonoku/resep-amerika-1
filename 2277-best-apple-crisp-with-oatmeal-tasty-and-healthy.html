Ingredients of Apple Crisp with Oatmeal

  1. Prepare of cooking apples,peeled & sliced.
  2. Prepare of oatmeal, uncooked.
  3. It's of flour.
  4. It's of packed brown sugar.
  5. You need of ground cinnamon.
  6. Prepare of butter,melted.

Apple Crisp with Oatmeal step by step

  1. preheat oven to 350°F..
  2. Place the apple slices in a 9x9-inch greased casserole dish..
  3. Combine oatmeal,flour, brown sugar, & cinnamon in a bowl. Mix melted butter with dry ingredients until crumbly..
  4. Sprinkle crumb mixture over apples..
  5. Bake for about 30 minutes, or until apples are tender..
  6. serve and enjoy.
