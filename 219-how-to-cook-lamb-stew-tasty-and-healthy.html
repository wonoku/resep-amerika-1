Ingredients of Lamb stew

  1. It's 1 kg of lamb chops.
  2. It's 250 ml of mushrooms.
  3. Prepare 250 ml of frozen vegetables.
  4. Prepare 1 of onion, chopped.
  5. It's 1 tsp of lamb rub.
  6. It's 1 tsp of lamb spice.
  7. It's 1 tsp of BBQ spice.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of chicken spice.
  9. It's 1/2 cube of chicken stock.
  10. You need 2 tbsp of minestrone powdered soup.
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp of canola oil.

Lamb stew instructions

  1. Rinse your lamb and boil for an hour until it's soft and cooked.
  2. Once it's done the drain the remaining water the add canola oil the let the meat fry and add onion and spices including the chicken stock.
  3. Let the mixture fry and then add mushrooms to cook cover the pot and let it cook..
  4. After cooking add those frozen vegetables and let them cook slowly until they are cooked.
  5. Pour the soups in a cup and mix with water. Then pour over the lamb chops lower the heat and let it cook. Serve once it's thick and tasty.
