Bread wheel chat (dabeli style) instructions

  1. Take a cooker, add potato, raw banana, Jackfruit seeds and little salt and cook it using water..
  2. Ingredients.
  3. Once it cools, mash them, add salt, green chilli and ginger paste,coriander leaves,fresh grated coconut, peanut chutney powder, turmeric powder, chat masala, asafoetida and grated mango and mix nicely. And make it ready into the tikki shape..

  6. Heat the pan, add little butter and shallow fry the stuffed bread..
  7. Take a bowl, add ketchup and mayonnaise and mix nicely, spread it to the edge of shallow fry bread. Now roll it on sev..
