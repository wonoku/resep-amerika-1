Ingredients of Radish chapati (mullangi masala chapati)

  1. Prepare 250 gms of radish.
  2. You need 2 cup of whole wheat flour.
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of oil.
  4. You need To taste of Salt according.
  5. You need 1/2 tbs of Asafoetida.
  6. It's 1 tbsp of red chilli powder.
  7. You need 1/2 tbs of turmeric powder.
  8. You need 2 tbsp of finely chopped green coriander leaves.
  9. It's 5 /6 of mint leaves finally chopped.
  10. Prepare 1 of green chilli finally chopped.
  11. It's 1/2 tbs of cumin seeds.
  12. It's As needed of Oil for making chapatis as necessary.

Radish chapati (mullangi masala chapati) instructions

  1. Wash and peel radish and shred with the help of shredder.
  2. In a bowl add all ingredients together mix all well and make dough with the help of some water if needed.
  3. Make dough and rest app 20 minutes to cover with lid or cotton cloth.
  4. Make small balls of dough and roll thin chapatis.
  5. Place tawa on gas when hot place chapati on it and cook it both sides by applying some oil from both sides.
  6. Radish chapati is ready for serve you can enjoy it with any veg or curd.
