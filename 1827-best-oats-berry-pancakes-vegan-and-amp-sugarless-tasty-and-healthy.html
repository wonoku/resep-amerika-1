Ingredients of Oats Berry Pancakes - Vegan & Sugarless

  1. It's of oats.
  2. Prepare of banana.
  3. It's of Apple sauce.
  4. It's of oats milk (can use any other plant based milk).
  5. You need of baking powder.
  6. It's of berries (can use fresh or frozen both. Do not thaw, if frozen).
  7. It's of Dressing:.
  8. Prepare of peanut butter.
  9. You need of berries.
  10. Prepare of crushed dates.
  11. You need of Water.
  12. It's of banana.

Oats Berry Pancakes - Vegan & Sugarless instructions

  1. Mash banana and add apple sauce. (I use homemade apple sauce always.).
  2. Add milk and then slowly add oats to make a flowing consistency..
  3. Add baking powder and mix well..
  4. Gently add berries and just fold in. Do not over mix them..
  5. Heat a pan and pour a ladle full to make a small circle. After 3-4 min, flip it over. Remove after 2 min..
  6. Dressing - add all except bananas and crush well in a blender. Add water to make it like syrup consistency. Cut bananas in chips and keep aside..
  7. Serving - Take a pancake on a plate. Place 4-5 banana chips on it. Keep another pancake on it. Decorate with bananas and pour syrup on it. Serve warm..
