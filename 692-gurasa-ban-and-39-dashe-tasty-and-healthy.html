Gurasa (Ban'dashe) step by step

  1. Sieve flour, add sugar and salt and mix..
  2. Activate yeast with warm water and pour in. Mix and knead until a soft dough is obtained.
  3. Cover and put in a warm place to rise for 30 minutes..
  4. After rising, apply some oil on Working area and cut dough into pieces and mold in balls..
  5. Get a pan (non-stick preferably) and apply 1 Tablespoon of oil and spread.
  6. Put the dough on top of the pan and spread with hands or spoon to give a fat circle.
  7. Cover and cook at very low heat.
  8. When cooked to Brown, flip other side as well.
  9. Cook all the remaining dough and set aside. Your Gurasa is ready..
  10. Season kuli-kuli powder and add oil to make a paste.
  11. Cut gurasa to small pieces.
  12. Mix gurasa with the kuli-kuli paste and mix thoroughly.
  13. Add Sliced vegetables and mix.
  14. Add more kuli-kuli powder.
  15. Serve and enjoy.
