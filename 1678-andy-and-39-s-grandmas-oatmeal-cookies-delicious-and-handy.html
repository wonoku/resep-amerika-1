andy's grandmas oatmeal cookies step by step

  1. preheat oven to 350°.
  2. cream together butter, oil, sugars, cinnamon, and apple pie spice..
  3. add egg and vanilla and mix well..
  4. Peel, core and finely dice apple and stir into mixture..
  5. add flour and stir..
  6. add oatmeal and stir.
  7. drop by spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheet and bake 7-10 min..
  8. cool slightly and remove from sheet onto cooling rack.
  9. these are very good "fully loaded", adding walnuts, choc. chips, raisins or dates, coconut, and /or pecans ..
