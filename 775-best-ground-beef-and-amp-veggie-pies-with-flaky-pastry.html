Ingredients of Ground Beef & Veggie Pies with Flaky Pastry

  1. You need of ground beef.
  2. You need of large onion, chopped.
  3. Prepare of carrot, finely chopped.
  4. Prepare of green pepper, chopped.
  5. You need of red pepper, chopped.
  6. It's of garlic cloves, chopped.
  7. You need of tomatoes, grated.
  8. You need of grated parmesan.
  9. Prepare of olive oil.
  10. It's of salt & freshly ground pepper.
  11. You need of pastry sheets.
  12. It's of melted margarine.
  13. It's of flour for rolling out.

Ground Beef & Veggie Pies with Flaky Pastry instructions

  1. Heat 80ml olive oil in a wide pan over medium high heat. Add the onion and stir for 2min..
  2. Add the beef and break it up with a fork until it becomes granular. When it turns grey, add the carrot. Keep stirring with a wooden spoon..
  3. Continue with the green and red pepper, the garlic and the tomatoes. Season with salt & pepper..
  4. When all the liquid has evaporated, remove from the heat. Add the parmesan, stir well and let the mixture cool completely..
  5. Put the first piece of dough onto your bench. Sprinkle with flour..
  6. Roll out a rectangular pastry sheet, 35 x 25cm / 13.7” x 9.8”. Brush the excess flour..
  7. Brush with melted margarine. Spread some filling in the middle and fold the left side..
  8. Spread some filling and fold the right side. Brush with melted margarine, fold in half and brush again..
  9. Transfer onto a paper lined baking tray and flip it. Flatten it with your hand and brush with melted margarine. Repeat the process once more..
  10. Roll out another pastry sheet and cut it in half. Take each half and cover the prepared pies. Flatten with your hands and set aside..
  11. Prepare another pastry sheet and brush with margarine. Put filling along the bottom edge and roll it into a log..
  12. Transfer onto a paper lined baking tray and repeat the process..
  13. Brush with margarine and prick them with a fork..
  14. Bake in a preheated oven, at 200⁰C / 400⁰F for 30-35 min until they’re golden brown. Enjoy!.
