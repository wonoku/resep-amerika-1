Jackfruit prawn curry instructions

  1. Heat oil and saute onion.
  2. Add garlic and ginger saute well till the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add tomato puree and saute well adding salt and turmeric powder.
  4. Add Cumin powder and red chilli powder and saute.

  6. Add prawns and saute well.
  7. Cover and simmer till everything get combined well.
  8. Add sugar and garam masala powder.
  9. Serve it with rice or chapati.
