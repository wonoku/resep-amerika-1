Ingredients of Not your Mama's potato salad!

  1. It's of salad.
  2. It's 5 small of potatoes (I like yukon gold or red).
  3. Prepare 5 of hard boiled eggs.
  4. Prepare 3 of green onions, thinly sliced.
  5. Prepare 1/2 of red bell pepper, diced.
  6. Prepare 3 slice of crispy bacon, diced.
  7. It's 1/4 of mayonnaise.
  8. You need 2 tbsp of course ground dijon mustard.
  9. It's 1 tsp of salt.
  10. It's 1 tsp of black pepper.
  11. You need 1/2 tsp of paprika.
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp of garlic powder.
  13. It's 1/2 tbsp of apple cider vinegar.

Not your Mama's potato salad! step by step

  1. Simmer potatoes on medium heat. Cook for about 20 min or so. (The skins are left on so it is a long cook time, but it keeps you from having soggy potatoes.).
  2. Halve eggs and remove the yolks, set yolks aside in a large mixing bowl. Dice the whites and set aside..
  3. Add the mayonnaise, mustard, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic, and vinegar to the bowl with the yolks and mix until smooth..
  4. Once the potatoes are done let them cool a bit and give them a large dice (I leave the skins on). Add to the mayo mix and let cool in the fridge..
  5. Once the potatoes are cooled add the egg, onion, bacon and peppers. Now its ready to eat!.
