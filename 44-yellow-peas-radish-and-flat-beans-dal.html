Yellow peas radish and flat beans dal step by step

  1. Heat oil and temper nigella seeds and red chilli.
  2. Add sliced radish and beans and saute well.
  3. Add salt and turmeric powder and cover till it is soft.
  4. Now pour the daal and mix well adding sugar.
  5. Serve it with rice.
