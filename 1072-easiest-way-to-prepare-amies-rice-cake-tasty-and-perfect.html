Ingredients of AMIEs RICE Cake

  1. You need 800 grams of glutinous rice (powder).
  2. It's 1/2 tsp of salt.
  3. Prepare 12 tbsp of sugar.
  4. It's 1 can of condensed milk.
  5. You need 1 medium of bottle preserved jackfruit (sliced) or sweet macapuno.
  6. You need 4 can of coconut milk.

AMIEs RICE Cake step by step

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C. Lightly grease an oblong pan..
  2. In a large heavy bowl, combine coconut milk, sugar, salt and rice. Mix it thoroughly until all ingredients are well incorpprated..
  3. Spread the mixture in a the greased 9"x13" oblong pan, spreading the mixture evenly. Bake for 20 minutes..
  4. Place in a wire rack. Combine the condensed milk with jackfruit or macapuno until well blended. Pour in pan, spreading the cover top evenly..
  5. Put again in the oven and bake for another 20 minutes, until light golden. Watch it carefully. It will burn easily..
  6. Set aside to cool. Serve, share and enjoy!.
