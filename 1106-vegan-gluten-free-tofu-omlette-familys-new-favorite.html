Ingredients of Vegan Gluten-Free Tofu Omlette

  1. It's of Egg Mix.
  2. You need 2 packages of Soft Mori-Nu Tofu.
  3. It's 2 tbsp of Golden Flax Seeds.
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp of Gluten Free Garbanzo Flour.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of Black Kavala Salt.
  6. It's 1/4 cup of Nutritional Yeast Flakes.
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp of Onion Powder.
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp of Garlic Powder.
  9. It's 1/4 tsp of Paprika.
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Turmeric.
  11. You need 1 dash of Chili powder.
  12. Prepare 1 tsp of Chicken Seasoning (vegetarian).
  13. Prepare 4 oz of Soy Kaas Mozzarella Cheese.
  14. You need 1 tbsp of extra virgin olive oil.
  15. You need of Omelette Fillings.
  16. Prepare 2 of Small White Onions.
  17. Prepare 1 of Red Pepper.
  18. Prepare 5 of White Mushrooms.
  19. You need 7 of Green Olives.
  20. It's 2 of Chive Onions.
  21. Prepare 4 clove of garlic.
  22. You need 1 of Large Tomato.

Vegan Gluten-Free Tofu Omlette step by step

  1. Lightly oil a pan with Extra Virgin Olive Oil and heat pan on medium high. Be sure to have your own sufficiently hot and ready.
  2. Combine all egg mix ingredients in a blender.
  3. Blend till smooth.
  4. Set egg mix aside and prepare your omelette fillings.
  5. Chop omelette fillings however you like, so they fit in the omelette.
  6. Pour your egg mix into your pre-heated pan, about half an inch thick.
  7. In another pan, combine your omelette fillings in another pan set to high. Leave out tomatoes from pan for now.
  8. Your egg mix will be ready to flip when the edges are golden brown. Flip carefully with a large spatula.
  9. Add fillings to your omelette, and allow the other side to cook a bit.
  10. Once cooked, remove omelette carefully and fold over once you place it on the plate.
