Mike's Kofta Kebobs instructions

  1. Make your Tatziki Sauce and let chill. My recipe can be found in the link below..
  2. Clean and preheat your gas grill or clean and light your charcoal grill. Authors Note: Charcoal will offer you much more flavor in your meat..
  3. Chop up and mix everything called for in the Kofta Meat section together very well. Refrigerate meat for at least one hour..
  4. Mold meat into five, 6"long x 1" wide, cigar shape forms as pictured..
  5. Mold meat around your scewers tightly using tap water to moisten the outside of your meat if needed..
  6. Grill meat for 8 - 10 minutes total. Try not to flip meat until the top side begins to firm and can hold up to a turn. Gently turn your meat [repeating] and grill until done..
  7. Grill Naan Bread until you can see score marks on the bread and a slight crisp. Do not over-grill as you'll harden your bread..
  8. Serve whole pieces of meat on Naan Bread with Tatziki Sauce, fresh Cilantro and any other additions listed..
