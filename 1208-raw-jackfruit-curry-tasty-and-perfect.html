Raw jackfruit curry instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan add the jackfruit and Potatoes saute well adding salt and turmeric powder.
  2. Remove the fried vegetables and add oil in the pan.
  3. Add jeera seeds bay leaf and red chilli Powder.
  4. Add onion slices saute well till it become golden brown.
  5. Add the ginger garlic paste and saute well adding salt and turmeric powder.
  6. Now add all the masalas saute well.
  7. Add the fried vegetables and saute well adding warm water.
  8. Cover it for 10 mnts or so.
  9. Finally add sugar and garam masala powder.
  10. Garnish it with ghee and serve.
