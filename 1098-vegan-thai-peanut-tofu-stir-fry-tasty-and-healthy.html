Ingredients of Vegan thai peanut tofu stir fry

  1. Prepare 2 tbsp of virgin coconut oil.
  2. You need 2 of green onions, sliced thin.
  3. Prepare 4 cloves of garlic, minced.
  4. Prepare 2 of mushrooms, sliced.
  5. You need 4 of sundried tomatoes, chopped.
  6. It's 1 cup of fresh basil, chopped.
  7. You need 1/4 cup of coconut flakes.
  8. You need 1/4 cup of cashews.
  9. Prepare 1 package of firm tofu, cubed.
  10. Prepare of Mr Spice Organic Thai Peanut Sauce.
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp of red or yellow curry.
  12. Prepare of water.

Vegan thai peanut tofu stir fry step by step

  1. Heat coconut oil in a large pan, and sautée the green onion, garlic, mushroom, and sundried tomatoes..
  2. After a few minutes, add basil, coconut flakes and cashews.
  3. Add tofu cubes, sauce, curry, add a bit of water. Let simmer until flavours are fully absorbed by the tofu.
