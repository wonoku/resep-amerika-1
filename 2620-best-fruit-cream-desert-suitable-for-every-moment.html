Fruit cream desert step by step

  1. Take a pan and add 4_5 spoons brown sugar in it with 1 ½ spoon water and make sheera utill turn in a crispy tofee.
  2. Now cut the fruits (which you like to eat) in small pieces..
  3. Add sugar in a cream and mix all the fruit in it. Add all dry fruits also in the cream..
  4. Take a bowl for serving. 1st of all put crispy tofee pieses and honey in the bottom of the bowl. Then add the layer of fruit cream on it..
  5. Now add green Asharfiaan in it and keep them aside of glass bowl with the help of straw..
  6. Now on the upper layer of bowl, add the crushed brown sugar tofee and Kishmish..
  7. Super duper fruit truffle in ready to enjoy..
