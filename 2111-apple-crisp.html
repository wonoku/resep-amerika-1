Ingredients of Apple Crisp

  1. You need 10 cup of Apples peeled, cored and sliced.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of White sugar.
  3. It's 1 tbsp of All purpose flour.
  4. It's 1 tsp of Ground cinnamon.
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup of Water.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of Quick cooking oats.
  7. It's 1 cup of All purpose flour.
  8. It's 1 cup of Packed brown sugar.
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp of Baking powder.
  10. It's 1/4 tsp of Baking soda.
  11. It's 1/2 cup of Butter melted.

Apple Crisp step by step

  1. Preheat oven to Preheat oven to 350°F (175 degree C)..
  2. Place the sliced apples in a 9x13 inch pan. Mix the white sugar, 1 tablespoon flour and ground cinnamon together, and sprinkle over apples. Pour water evenly over all..
  3. Combine the oats, 1 cup flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda and melted butter together. Crumble evenly over the apple mixture..
  4. Bake at 350°F (175 degrees C) for about 45 minutes..
