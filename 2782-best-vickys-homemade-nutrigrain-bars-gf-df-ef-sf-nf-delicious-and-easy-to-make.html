Ingredients of Vickys Homemade Nutrigrain Bars, GF DF EF SF NF

  1. You need of gluten-free / plain flour (1 & 1/4 cups).
  2. It's of soft brown sugar (2/3 cup).
  3. Prepare of rolled oats (1 rounded cup).
  4. It's of baking powder.
  5. You need of xanthan gum if using GF flour.
  6. It's of sunflower oil or melted safe 'butter' (1/4 cup).
  7. It's of apple juice (1/4 cup).
  8. Prepare of jam of choice for filling - strawberry, apricot, blueberry.

Vickys Homemade Nutrigrain Bars, GF DF EF SF NF instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to gas 3 / 160C / 325F and line an 8"Ã— 12" tin.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients together.
  3. Add the oil / melted safe fat and juice. Mix to form a slightly crumbly dough.
  4. Press half of the mixture into the bottom of the tin. You may need to flour your hands.
  5. Spread the jam on top.
  6. Gently press the remaining oat mixture on top. This can be a bit tricky! Flour your hands well! It may be easier to use a rolling pin if you want to then lay the top layer over the jam.
  7. Bake for 35 - 40 minutes until golden.
  8. Let cool in the tin then turn out and cut into slices.
