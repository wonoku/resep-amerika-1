Apple Strusel Cheesecake instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350. Spray a 9 inch springform pan with bakers spray. Line a baking sheet with foil.
  2. Make Crust.
  3. Combine cinnamon grahm crumbs and butter until blended.
  4. Press into springform pan, bottom and sides. Freeze while preparing filling.
  5. Make Apple Filling.
  6. Melt butter in a small skillet, add apple slices, lemon and cook on medium heat just until starting to soften, about 5 minutes.
  7. Add sugar and cinnamon and cook until thick but apple still has some shape, cool to room temperature.
  8. Make Cheesecake.
  9. In a large bowl beat cream cheese until smooth.
  10. Add sugar and beat well until light and fluffy, beat in lemon, apple pie spice and vanilla.
  11. Add eggs one at a time and beat in after each egg.
  12. Fold in sour cream and heavy cream until completely blended.
  13. Pour half of cheesecake batter over crust.
  14. Add apple filling, swirling it over layer.
  15. Add remaing cheesecake batter smoothing top over apples and place on foil lined pan and bake 45 minutes.

  17. Meanwhile Prepare Crumb Topping.
  18. In a bowl mix together flour, light brown sugar, granulated sugar and cinnamon.
  19. Add melted butter and combine with a fork into crumbs.
  20. After the cheesecake bakes 45 minutes, remove from oven and evenly add crumb topping to top of cheesecake. Return to oven and cook an additional 35 minutes.
