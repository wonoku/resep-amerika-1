Vegan Gingerbread men cookies step by step

  1. Blend dry all the ingredients listed under “to be blended dry”.
  2. Pack the dough in a cling film and store in refrigerator overnight.
  3. Sprinkle the working board with some flour and lay your dough on it and flatten it first with your hands.
  4. Place the dough between 2 wax papers/baking sheet.Then gently roll it out circular using a rolling pin.
  5. Cut of ginger man cookies using cookie cutter.
  6. Lay the cut of cookies on a baking tray and bake for 10-15 minutes at 200 degree celcius.
  7. Let cool, while we prepare ganache and cookie dressing.
  8. Whip 10 gms of thick coconut creme and scoop out in a piping bag and draw ginger men’s facial features, buttons, etc.
  9. In another bowl whip 10 gms coconut creme and spinach purée and prepare thick ganache for frosting.
  10. You can choose to cover either one side of whole cookie or only create certain features like buttons, etc using this ganache.
  11. Use strawberry crush for red colour ganache.
  12. Refrigerate for 10-15 minutes for the ganache to set.
  13. Enjoy your Christmas and surprise your guests with these pretty gingerbread men cookies.
  14. Tip:The cookies taste awesome without the frosting, but you may choose to decorate them for the holiday season..
  15. Tip:Refrigeration process can be for 8 hours too at the minimum.
