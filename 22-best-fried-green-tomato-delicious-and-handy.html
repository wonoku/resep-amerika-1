Ingredients of Fried Green Tomato

  1. It's 1 cup of flour.
  2. You need 1 cup of seasoned corn meal.
  3. Prepare 1 of egg whipped.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of salt.
  5. Prepare 1 large of Green Tomato.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of canola oil.
  7. It's 1 tbsp of butter.

Fried Green Tomato instructions

  1. Cut tomato in slices..
  2. Lay slices flat and sprinkle with salt..
  3. Coat with flour..
  4. Dip in beaten egg..
  5. Then coat in seasoned cornmeal..
  6. Heat oil over meadium heat in frying pan. Place butter in oil..
  7. When the butter has melted and begins to sizzle the oil is hot enough. Brown each side til golden brown..
