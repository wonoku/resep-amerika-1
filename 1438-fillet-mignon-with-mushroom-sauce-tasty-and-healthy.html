Fillet mignon with mushroom sauce instructions

  1. Marinate beef steak with only salt and pepper and olive oil and rest it for 2-3 hours..
  2. Make the grill pan very hot but do not grease the pan,grease the meat with olive oil and place in on hot pan..
  3. Grill for 3-4 min than flip after flipping put a spoon of butter and cloves of garlic for seasoning and grill for 2-3 min..
  4. Plate the steak and set with side lines..
  5. For veggies :cut veggies in Julian style and stir fry with olive oil and butter add seasoning (salt,pepper,thyme).
  6. For mushroom sauce:heat the pan and add butter than add flour mix well and add milk whisk continuously try to do not make lumps. Add little water as required and then add seasoning (salt,pepper,chicken powder,thyme,garlic powder)now add chopped mushrooms and mix well..
  7. Set the plate steak plate with veggies,baked potatoes or fries (your choice)caramelised garlic,mushroom sauce and dips..
