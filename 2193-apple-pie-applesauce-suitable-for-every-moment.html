Ingredients of Apple Pie applesauce

  1. You need of about 15 apples, peeled and cored (I use wild apples various sizes).
  2. You need of water.
  3. Prepare of brown sugar.
  4. It's of lemon juice.
  5. It's of ground cinnamon.
  6. It's of packets spiced apple cider mix.
  7. Prepare of to 15 caramel cubes (unwrapped).
  8. You need of vanilla extract.

Apple Pie applesauce step by step

  1. Fill crockpot to the top with apples. Add all ingredients. Cook on low heat for 3 to 5 hrs.
  2. Whisk apples in about 3 hrs . Ready when mushed easily and thickens..
  3. Pour in jars and place in freezer or fridge. Will last about a week in the fridge..
  4. NOTE*** every batch I have made, has been slightly adjusted. Based on apple sweetness, and personal taste. This is a baseline recipe. Feel free to add smaller amounts of sugar items, until you get the flavor you want :).
