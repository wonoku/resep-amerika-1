Mandys chicken goujons (tenders) instructions

  1. Heat oven to 190C/375F. Arrange a breading station.
  2. Have chicken tenders ready, then dip into seasoned eggs in a bowl..
  3. Then place chicken into a zip lock bag a couple at a time and coat well with seasoned breadcrumbs..
  4. Repeat the steps, egg and then breadcrumbs to ensure a good coating. Allow the coating to slightly dry for 5 minutes..
  5. Place chicken on a foil line cooking sheet, bake for 20-25 minutes until lightly golden and fully cooked..
  6. Remove from tray and served, great just dipped into Ur favourite sauce, with a salad or in a tortilla wrap and enjoy. The choice is endless..
