Bisi Bela Bath instructions

  1. Roast the Masala and Grind to a smooth paste.
  2. Pressure cook Rice, dal, vegetables in 5 cups of water for 4 whistles.
  3. Soak Tamarind and and extract the juice.
  4. Add Ground Masala, Tamarind juice, salt into the cooked rice and dal.
  5. Let this gets cooked in medium heat for 3 to 4 mins.
  6. Season with mustard, jeera, fenugreek, red chilli, curry leaves and urad dal.
  7. Finally garnished with ghee and coriander leaves.
  8. Ready to Serve.
