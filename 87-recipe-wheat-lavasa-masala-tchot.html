Wheat Lavasa Masala Tchot step by step

  1. Add salt to flour and mix it. Add dry yeast and knead flour with warm water. Add oil and rest for 20-30 minutes..
  2. Soak white peas for 5-6 hours. Add turmeric, salt, black pepper and cloves. Boil it until cooked. Approximately 7-8 whistles in cooker. Cool it and drain off water. Mix it thoroughly into dry paste..
  3. Radish Chutney - Add all ingredients to chutney jar and grind to fine chutney..
  4. Knock out air and make equal size balls out of it. Roll it evenly and brush it t with water on one side. On hot tava lay the waterside and stick Lavasa on tava. When one side is done, cook the other side direct on flame. When Lavasa is ready, spread spicy radish chutney evenly. Apply white peas mixture. Roll it nicely and serve it..
  5. Enjoy Masaledaar tchot. Happy eating..
