Jackfruit biriyani step by step

  1. Heat sufficient oil in a pan and deep fry the jackfruit pieces until they turn a light brown in colour.
  2. Heat water in a non-stick pan and add bay leaves, green and brown cardamom, cloves, cinnamon and black cumin.
  3. Now, add the rice and enough salt and bring to a boil and cook until the rice is half done.
  4. Drain the rice and keep aside.
  5. In a deep bottomed non-stick pan, mix yogurt, ¾ cup fried onions, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, ginger strips, green chillies, ginger garlic paste, salt, 1 tbsp ghee, fried jackfruit, and 1 tsp garam masala powder.
  6. Add 1 tsp cardamom powder, 1 tbsp coriander leaves and 8-10 mint leaves and mix well.
  7. Add ¼ cup of boiling water and mix well.
  8. Mix the saffron with 2 tsp boiling water, 2 tsp milk and 2 tsp rose water Take half the cooked rice and spread it over the yogurt mixture. Sprinkle a pinch of garam masala powder and green cardamom powder each.
  9. Add 7-8 mint leaves, coriander leaves, the remaining fried onion, ghee and ½ of the prepared saffron milk.
  10. Strain the remaining rice and spread it over the added layer..
  11. Strew 7-8 mint leaves and the remaining saffron milk over the rice layer..
  12. Add some ghee. Cover and cook on high heat for first 2 minutes. Then lower the heat and cook for 12-15 minutes till the rice is done. Transfer into a serving bowl and serve hot..
