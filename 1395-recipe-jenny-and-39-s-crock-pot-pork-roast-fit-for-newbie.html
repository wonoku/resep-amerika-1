Ingredients of Jenny's Crock Pot Pork Roast

  1. It's of Pork Tenderloin.
  2. You need of Carrots (Peeled and cut into 2 to 3 in. pieces).
  3. Prepare of Gold or New Potatoes (Quartered).
  4. It's of Onion (Red or Yellow).
  5. It's of Lipton Onion Soup Mix (1 Packet).
  6. You need of Chicken Bouillon.
  7. It's of medium Bay Leaf.
  8. It's of Thyme.
  9. It's of Garlic (Minced).
  10. Prepare of Water (Filtered).
  11. Prepare of Olive Oil.
  12. Prepare of Garlic Powder.
  13. You need of Dried Parsley.
  14. You need of Salt.
  15. You need of Black Pepper.

Jenny's Crock Pot Pork Roast step by step

  1. Wash and prepare vegetables. Set aside..
  2. Preheat Olive Oil in a non-stick skillet to Medium/High heat..
  3. Season Pork Tenderloin with Garlic Powder, Salt, Pepper, and Dried Parsley..
  4. Brown Pork in skillet. Be sure to brown all sides of the tenderloin..
  5. Place Tenderloin into a large Crock Pot..
  6. Layer the Vegetables over the Pork..
  7. Sprinkle Lipton Onion Soup Mix over the Vegetables..
  8. Add Minced Garlic Clove, Thyme, Chicken Bouillon, and Bay Leaf..
  9. Pour the 6 cups of Water in Crock Pot, and cover..
  10. Cook for 8 to 10 hours..
  11. If you desire more of a gravy, you can add some flour to the liquid, prior to cooking..
