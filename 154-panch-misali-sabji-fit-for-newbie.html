Panch misali sabji step by step

  2. Add five spices and green chillies.
  3. Now add the vegetables and saute well adding salt and turmeric powder.
  4. Add ginger paste and saute well adding red chilli Powder.
  5. Now cover and cook till it is soft.
  6. Add roasted masala powder and sugar.
  7. Serve it with khichdi.
