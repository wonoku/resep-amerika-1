chocolate chip shortbread fruit pizza instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. add all your cookie ingredients together, will be cookie consistency. now press to bottom of pizza pan, bake for 22-26 min will be golden on sides and still semi soft in middle..
  3. whip your whipping cream, pudding, sugar & vanilla together till its whipped to a semi thick cream. Now add your cream cheese and whip till smooth, now add your tub of cool whip till incorporated. ..
  4. set your whip topping in fridge for 30 minutes while your cookie cools. once cooled add your whip topping leaving a space around the edges. Add your fruit to desired design. Brush your fruit with your orange marmalade chill till ready to serve..
