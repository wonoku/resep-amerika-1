Ingredients of Yummy Breaded Baked Chicken

  1. It's 2 c of Andy's yellow fish breading.
  2. It's 1 tbs of garlic powder.
  3. You need 1 tsp of Himalayan pink salt.
  4. It's 1 c of Shredded Parmesan asiago romano cheese.
  5. You need 1 c of Helmans mayo.
  6. Prepare 1 package of boneless skinless chicken tenders.

Yummy Breaded Baked Chicken step by step

  1. Mix together breading, cheese, garlic, salt. Set aside.
  2. Tenderize chicken breasts.
  3. Roll in mayo.
  4. Toss in breading.
  5. Place in an oven safe pan and bake for 45 minutes at 400 degrees.
